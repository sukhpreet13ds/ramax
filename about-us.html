ABOUT US

We understand that your lifestyle changes, that's why we have made fitness straightforward and stress free. Join us today on a no lock-in contract membership and start achieving your fitness goals. We value flexibility at Ramax Gym, with unlimited 24/7 access.

Ramakrishnan (RK) is a Strength & Conditioning Coach with over 7 years of coaching experience, specializing in injury rehabilitation, obesity management, pre- and post-natal fitness, and athletic performance training.

He has worked with diverse populations through platforms such as HealthifyMe, FitMom Club, and independent coaching, helping clients move from pain, limitation, and inconsistency to strength, control, and confidence.

RJ’s approach is built on a simple principle:

”Strength should solve problems, not create them.”

His coaching integrates movement science, progressive strength training, and real-world application, ensuring every individual trains according to their body, history, and goals — not a one-size-fits-all program.

With his first enterprise studio, Ramakrishnan brings a coach-led, functional training environment focused on long-term health, injury-free progression, and performance-based results.
